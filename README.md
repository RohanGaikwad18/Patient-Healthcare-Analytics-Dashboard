🎯 Objective
Analyze patient and hospital data
Track key healthcare KPIs
Identify trends in billing, admissions, and medical conditions
Provide a clear and interactive dashboard for decision-making
📊 Key Metrics (KPIs)
Total Patients: 56K
Average Billing Amount: 25.54K
Average Age: 51.54
Average Length of Stay: 15.51 days
Abnormal Test Count: 19K
📈 Dashboard Features
Top 10 Hospitals by Revenue
Identify high-performing hospitals based on revenue generation
Year-wise Filtering
Analyze trends across multiple years (2019–2024)
Gender-wise Patient Distribution
Compare male vs female patient ratios
Insurance Provider Analysis
Breakdown of insured patients by provider
Medical Condition Analysis
Track patient count across diseases like Diabetes, Cancer, Asthma, etc.
🧰 Tools & Technologies Used
Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)

Data Cleaning & Transformation

The dataset includes:

Patient demographics (Age, Gender)
Hospital information
Billing amount
Admission & discharge dates
Insurance provider
Medical conditions
Length of stay
🧠 Key Insights
Certain hospitals contribute significantly higher revenue than others
Billing patterns vary across years and patient types
Insurance providers play a major role in patient coverage
Chronic conditions like Diabetes and Hypertension have higher patient counts
